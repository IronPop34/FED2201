I was born in the United States and spent most of my childhood with my family abroad in Europe and the Middle East. I discovered that I had Dyslexia and ADD during this time. With the different cultures, languages, and religious experiences in my daily childhood life, I was able to expand my perception of the world. Faced with the additional challenges of Dyslexia and ADD, with hard work I managed to graduate high school while also finding my talent in the Creative Art field. I returned to the United States, achieved an Associate degree in Fine Art, and then attended SCAD University and achieved a Bachelor’s Fine Arts degree in Animation. After graduation, like many other millennial people, I had to persevere and carry on in order to materialize my own expectations and dreams; despite setbacks.

To date, I have been featured in “On Media Signals and Connections” in Rome Italy (website LoosenArt.com) and am having his first solo exhibition in Lenore, North Carolina in July 2022.
